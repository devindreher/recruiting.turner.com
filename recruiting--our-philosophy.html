Why we're here

The recruiting team is a pack of hunters dedicated to finding the best people out there to work at Turner.


We thrive on the challenge of the mission:

Be storytellers of great brands and the culture that built them.

Challenge the status quo to reinvent — not recycle.

Be ultimate matchmakers for great people and great jobs.

Find the best in diverse places and faces.

Our Philosophy

Challenging the Status Quo

If we want to re-imagine TV, we must re-imagine our workforce.

Hiring the same kind of employee over and over again only yields the same result, and we know that same result won't put us on top.

We want to challenge the status quo by pushing hiring managers to think differently about every hire. Different backgrounds, experiences, and skills. Differences that will raise the performance of your entire team with new energy and ideas.

Headcount is precious. If every hiring manager aimed to make their next extraordinary, image where we can go.

Storytelling

Recruiters are the first contact that a candidate has with us about a job.

It's easy to sell a candidate on a job they applied for, but it's a different game to sell someone on a job they didn't.

That happens with storytelling. It begins with branding — something as simple as a job description that makes someone smile because they "get it." A compelling post on social media that makes someone want to know more about us. An attractive job site that engages a candidate in more than just the job posting.

We do it for every job.

Sourcing

The recruiting team isn't huge, but it can be. Everyone is a recruiter!

We are engaging employees (that's you) more than ever through our referral program that pays a minimum of $1,000. Bring more people like you!

With every role, we're leveraging social media and membership networks of the hiring manager and anyone who will repost or retweet for us.

We're going where the candidates are and who better to tell us than you.

Compensation

Our compensation team is a group of subject matter experts who understand the art of pricing a job.

Our approach keeps them connected in real time. We share the expectations of candidates with them to help us be more competitive.

This strengthened partnership gives us edge when we need it to get the best person in the job.
