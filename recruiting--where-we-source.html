Where We Source

In today's competitve market, we can't depend on job postings alone to generate a pool of qualified candidates.

We have in-house "talent sourcers" tasked with enhancing our ability to identify, profile, and assess constantly changing streams of talent. Equipped with the latest prospecting techniques and tools used for information retrieval, data extraction, and web-scraping, our sourcers identify leads from various places.

Our talent sourcers reach is unbounded! They push the creative limits to identify talent for our business.
