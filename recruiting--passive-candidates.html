Passive Candidates

Passive candidates are those that did not apply for a job. These are folks found through your network or ours, or we contacted them directly to get them interested. Passive candidates need to be contacted immediately.

Passive candidates have jobs and are happy with their current employer. We did a little wooing and got them to talk to you. If you don't engage quickly, their interest may dissolve.

Your recriter will identify passive candidates and coach you on the best way to engage them based on their level of interest.

Things to Remember
